What is Customer Life Time Value ? 				

It is the total amount of revenue a business can expect to earn from a customer over the entire period they remain a customer.				

It is calculates as => Average amount a customer pay * Average no. of transaction * no. of years customer active 

Pros:

1 - It tells us the behaviour of our customers that helps us to plan how much to spend to acquire customers.

2 - it is Helpful to project the revenue for business planning.
